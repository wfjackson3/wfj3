Willis F. Jackson III is the founder of ShownHome, a startup building software to save real estate agents time.

His other activities include curating the StartupDigest for the Silicon Prairie region, contributing writer for Silicon Prairie News, and lead writer for We are KC Tech.

Willis studied electrical engineering in college, specializing in integrated circuit design, but has since moved on to teaching himself to program. The majority of his time is spent learning more about the world of business with a little bit of economics thrown in for good measure. He loves to get to know new people but finds meeting them to be the most challenging part. A hustler at heart, he enjoys solving systemic problems in creative ways. He insists that all engineers have a responsibility to use their superpowers to make positive change in the world. He very strongly believes that we need more engineers to run for public office. You can usually find him rejecting reality and trying to build his vision instead.
