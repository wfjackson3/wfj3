About Willis F. Jackson III

Willis is the founder of ShownHome, a startup building software to save real estate agents time.

Willis studied electrical engineering in college, specializing in integrated circuit design, but has since moved on to teaching himself to program. The majority of his time is spent learning more about the world of business with a little bit of economics thrown in for good measure. A hustler at heart, he enjoys solving systemic problems in creative ways.

He has contributed extensively to the KC startup community. His vision for the community centers around family He brought StartupDigest to the Silicon Prairie region and was one of the first contributing writers for Silicon Prairie News in KC.

Willis insists that all engineers have a responsibility to use their superpowers to make positive change in the world. He very strongly believes that we need more engineers to run for public office. You can usually find him rejecting reality and trying to build his vision instead. He loves to get to know new people but finds meeting them to be the most challenging part, so feel free to connect below.
