About Willis F. Jackson III

Willis is the Director of Sales and Marketing for Databasically, a company dedicated to bettering lives through technology that just happens to make software for other companies.

Willis has an engineering background but prefers the challenges of sales and marketing. He is a skilled operations manager with a knack for helping smaller organizations transition into larger, healthier organizations.

He has contributed lots of his personal time to the Kansas City startup community. His vision for the community centers around family. He brought StartupDigest to the Silicon Prairie region and was one of the first contributing writers for Silicon Prairie News in KC.

Willis really believes in the power of core values, so he made his public. He won't even consider working closely with people who haven't read Tribal Leadership. He loves to get to know new people, especially if he can do something to help them on their journey, so feel free to connect below.
